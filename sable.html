Sable

Sable is a female hedgehog and the older sister of Mabel
Sable is a shy and reserved type of person
and she prefers to work unnoticed while Mabel deals with the customers.

Sable is also great friends with Tom Nook.

What's Sable's role in New Horizons?

Sable currently just works at the sewing machine
like in the older games and you can also befriend her like then.
Upon befriending her, which takes about a week,
she will give you fabric, that you can use to wear or put on furniture.
