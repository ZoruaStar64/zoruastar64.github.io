Isabelle

Isabelle is a female dog from the Shih Tzu species
Isabelle is extremely respectful and friendly to others she is however very clumsy and absentminded
She does not like confrontation and prefers to settle things peacefully

Isabelle is a Secretary in the two latest animal crossing games (New Leaf and New Horizons)
in New Horizons she can be found in the resident services building with Tom Nook
She's often seen doing tasks inside the building and sometimes working out with Tom Nook.

Isabelle's tasks inside the resident services building are as follows:

  • Changing your island tune (this changes the little song that plays when talking to certain villagers)
  • Changing your island flag (this changes the design of your island's flag
    which is seen by anyone who happens to enter your island
  • Force villagers to change their outfits and catchphrases
