The basics about island life

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is all about deserted island life with your fellow animal friends.
Forage, fish, leap, climb and craft your way into a nice life on your very own island!

But you might get weary of spending your time on island in a mere tent yes?
Well good news! by racking up enough Bells (which are the currency in Animal Crossing) you can pay off a no interest debt!
and upgrade your tent into a splendid house!

By merely spending time on your island you can earn Nook Miles!
but i can hear you ask "what are Nook Miles and what do they do?" good question!
Nook Miles are a seperate currency from Bells you earn by completing specific challenges.
You are able to spend your Nook Miles inside Resident Services to obtain special goodies! (interested yet?)

By spending Nook Miles to get a Nook Miles ticket you can go to another Deserted island
and grab some goodies from there wether it be Fruit, Fish or anything else you see there it's yours!
