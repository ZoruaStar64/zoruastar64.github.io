Orville

Tom Nook is a special character who the player interacts with throughout the entire game inside of the resident services tent/building.
Tom Nook is the person who came up with the idea for the Island getaway package and the Nookphone.

The player is able to talk to Tom Nook at all times inside the resident services tent/building.
and ask him a couple of questions.

Among these questions are "what should i do?" in which Tom gives the player advice on what to do or gives the player a story related task such as finding a place for a building.

When the player has upgraded the resident services tent to a building Isabelle will move in there and Tom will have new dialogue and things you can do with him.
Among these new things are building ramps/stairs and bridges to have easier access on your island,
however if you do this you will have to slowly but surely donate money to lloid and once the bell requirement is met lloid will have the bridge/incline finished the next day first minute.
