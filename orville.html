Orville

Orville is a male dodo who is accompanied by his colleague Wilbur who is also a male dodo.
They work together at Dodo Airlines to bring the player to islands or to bring others
to the player's island.

Multiplayer

When inside the Dodo Airlines building the player can talk to orville
to do many different things with one of those being Local/Online multiplayer.
The player can send mail to other players and attach items to them to send presents
and to send the other player a nice message.
You can also talk to Orville to make him open the gates to your island
this way other players can visit your island.
The player however MUST choose between Local or Online multiplayer
the player can't have a local friend playing with an online friend at the same time.
The player can tell Orville that they want to visit a friend (either Local or Online)
at that point the player connects to the visiting player's device and visit their island.

Singleplayer

In singleplayer circumstances if the player has a Nook Miles Ticket
they will be able to go on a tour once selecting the option at Orville.
at that point Orville will contact Wilbur to send the player towards a tour island.
