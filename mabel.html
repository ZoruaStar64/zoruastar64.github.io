Mabel

Mabel is a female Hedgehog who works at the able sisters clothing store.
Mable gets along with her sister Sable but has arguements from time to time (which get resolved quickly).

Mable in New Horizons

After a couple in real life days of playing New Horizons Mable has a chance to appear
in the island square infront the resident services.

After buying a certain amount of items from her she asks the player
to find her a suitable place for her store.
it will take two in real life days for the able sisters shop to be built.

Once the able sisters store is built Sable will move in together with Mable.
Mable will sell the player a randomised selection of clothes each day.
It's easier an better to buy clothes from the fitting room to the right of the building.
