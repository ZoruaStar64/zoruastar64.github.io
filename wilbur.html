Wilbur

Wilbur is a male dodo who works at Dodo Airlines with his colleague Orville.

What does Wilbur do?

Wilbur currently only does the singleplayer portion of the game
and is seen in a cutscene of the multiplayer rides to other islands
he is also seen at the end of a cutscene when you arrive back on your own island

on island tours he is seen waiting beside the airplane
when talking to him he gives you three dialogue options
The top option being to return back to your island and finishing the tour.
the middle option will be asking him for new tools
This will cost you 100 Nook miles every tool.
The bottom option being a cancel option to get back to your tour.

it's also to be assumed that he is the one talking to the player
when in the plane cutscenes during the opening and Multiplayer cutscenes.
