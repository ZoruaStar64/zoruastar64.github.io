The Nookphone

The Nookphone is an incredible invention made by Tom Nook himself!
The Nookphone has many apps installed on it which the player uses throughout the game.

The Nookphone's apps

The apps which the Nookphone currently has are essential to your island life
such as an app for crafting recipes, your fish/bug collection and a map!
Here is a list of all the apps found on your Nookphone and what they can do!

The Camera

The camera app can take pictures
while using this app you can also edit how the pictures look
You can zoom in and out, change up the picture's style
and give it barriers around the taken picture.

Nook Miles

In the Nook Miles aplication you can see many challenges
which you can complete to earn Nook Miles
You can later get an upgraded version of this app called Nook Miles+
Which adds extra challenges that keep renewing.

Critterpedia

In the Critterpedia app you can see a collection of all bugs/fish you collected
when selecting a critter you can view lots of usefull information such as:
at which times it spawns (months, hours), a fully detailed model of the critter
and wether or not you have donated it to Blathers.

D.I.Y recipes

Inside this app the player will be able to view all the D.I.Y recipes they learned.
and what the player needs to craft them(no seriously that's it)

Tom Nook Shopping Catalogue

The player unlocks the Catalog app after spending over 100.000 bells
In the Nook Shopping portion of the Nook Stop machine
This app allows free access to the Nook Shopping catalogue wherever the player is.

Island Creator

Using this app the player can terraform their island
if they have the required permits
This allows for (almost) free customization of the player's island
With the exception of Buildings and beaches.

Custom Designs

The player can make their own clothes using this app
This starts off as just basic T-shirts and face-paint but,
once the player purchases Pro Designs from the Nook Stop with 800 Nook Miles
the player gets access to a bunch of new clothing templates
Those being Tops, Dresses and Headwear.

Map

This app gives a map of the entire island
it also shows where all the buildings/houses are located.

Chat

This app gets unlocked once you play Multiplayer for the first time
using this app you can view a chatlog and chat with others players.

Passport

In this app the Player can customize their public passport in many ways
They can set a message, they can set a title for themself
And they can change their passport picture by taking a picture of themself.

Call Resident

This app gets unlocked when
when multiple switch account on the same device have played New-Horizons,
with this app they can call those accounts into the player's island
to help the player with tasks (this is a form of multiplayer that uses ONE switch)

Rescue Service

This app is used to take the player out of an area
that they're unable to get out of
there are multiple destinations the player can choose then which are:
The Airport, Resident Services Building and the Player's house.
However to use this feature the player needs to spend 100 Nook Miles.

Best Friends

This app is used for island permissions among your friends/visitors
This toggles wether or not they can place/take stuff on your island.
