Harvey

Harvey is a male spaniel breed dog who resembles a stereotypical hippie.
which is why he acts absentminded, loves nature and animals and doesn't like technology.
He is also vegan

What does Harvey do in New Horizons?

In New Horizons Harvey first meets up with the player
once they've gotten their third villager to move in.
Harvey arrives on the players island to compliment them
for the rapid development of the player's island and explains
that he lives by himself on another island nearby to which he refers to as "Harv's Island"
after which he invites the player to visit his island.

at Harv's Island the player can enter Photopia, which is a free photography studio Harvey made
The player can decorate several rooms of the house with their catalog's furniture
they can also invite other villagers to assist as models (and give them clothes for the photo)

Harvey also has no music associated with him or his island
making it the only place in New Horizons (so far) without music.
