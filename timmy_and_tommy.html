Timmy and Tommy

Timmy and Tommy are two brothers who work under Tom Nook and are NOT his kids or nephews.
However, Tom Nook did adopt them and does think of them as his pupils while they refer to Tom as their mentor.

What they do in New Horizons

At the start of New Horizons they can be seen at an airport waiting
for the ones that bought the island getaway package to arrive.
They start by asking basic questions for the start of any game,
like: how your character looks like and what thing they'd bring with them on a deserted island.

After the tutorial day on the island you can buy items from Timmy and sell items to Timmy.
Meanwhile Tommy can be seen waving a flag and walking around the island.

Once Nook's Cranny is built, Timmy and Tommy will move in there
and work in there selling wares to you and buying stuff from you.

Once Nook's Cranny is built they will only sell/buy during the service hours
of 8am to 10pm (you can still sell at the drop-off box in front of the store).
