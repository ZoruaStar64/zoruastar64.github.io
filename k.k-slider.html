K.K Slider

K.K Slider is a male Jack Russel breed dog
Who's also well known in and outside the animal crossing universe.
He has a total of 291 songs from the original animal crossing to new leaf.
K.K Slider is a hippie-style traveling musician, this is reflected in his speech
He comes across somebody who wants to be liberated,
which is why he doesn't put prices on his music and offers to perform for others.

What does K.K Slider do in New Horizons?

In New Horizons after upgrading the Resident Services building,
Tom Nook will give the player a goal of improving the island
This is to convince K.K to play a concert on your island.
Once the required 3 star rating is met K.K will arrive the following day
after that he will arrive every saturday
to play a concert in the evening.

The player may request songs and like in the previous games
the player will recieve a copy of the first song they request.
On the player's birthday (or the saturday before) they can request K.K to play K.K. Birthday
Which sounds almost exactly like the in real life song "Happy birthday to you"
