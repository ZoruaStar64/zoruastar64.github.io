Blathers

Blathers is a male owl and the island's Museum Director
Blathers is as most owls a nocturnal animal and is seen sleeping during the day
The player however can wake him up to donate to the museum.

When Tom Nook gets a call from Blathers the player is asked by Tom Nook
if the player can find 5 different kinds of fish or bugs so Blathers comes to the island

When Blathers arrives he resides in a tent and he asks the player to get him
15 different kinds of fish, bugs or fossils.
afterwards construction on the actual museum building will start

once the Museum is built the player can once again donate to it
and they can now check out exhibits for each of the 3 categories
them being fish, bugs and fossils.

Blathers loves to blather about fossils, fish and bugs.
Just to note Blathers HATES bugs and is scared of the most harmless of all
aside from that fact he is still knoledgable on the topic of them.

Blathers also has a sister called Celeste who comes to the island
as a special visitor(character) and gives the player a recipe
for either a wand or a zodiac inspired furniture piece.
